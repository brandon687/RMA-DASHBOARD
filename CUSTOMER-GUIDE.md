# Customer RMA Guide

**How to Submit a Return Request to SCal Mobile**

Simple step-by-step guide for customers submitting device returns or repairs.

---

## Quick Overview

Need to return devices? Here's what you'll do:

1. Go to the RMA Portal
2. Select your customer type (US or International)
3. Review the return guidelines
4. Fill out the form with your information
5. Upload device details (Excel, CSV, or other files)
6. Get your reference number
7. Wait for approval (2 business days)
8. Ship devices once approved

**Portal URL**: https://scal-rma-portal.railway.app

---

## Before You Start

### What You'll Need

1. **Company Information**:
   - Company name
   - Company email address
   - Original sales order number

2. **Device Information**:
   - Excel or CSV file with device details
   - IMEIs (15-digit numbers starting with 35)
   - Device models and storage
   - Condition grades
   - Issue descriptions

3. **Supporting Documents** (optional but recommended):
   - Original invoice (PDF)
   - Photos of devices
   - Videos of issues

### Important Deadlines

- **Submit within**: 45 days of invoice date
- **Ship devices**: Within 10 days of RMA approval
- **Approval time**: 2 business days

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### Step 1: Access the Portal

1. Open your web browser
2. Go to: **https://scal-rma-portal.railway.app**
3. You'll see the SCal Mobile landing page
4. Click **"Enter Portal"** button

### Step 2: Select Customer Type

Choose your location:

#### US Customers
- You're based in the United States
- Will ship to: Irvine, CA
- Shipping timeline: Within 10 days of approval

**Click "Select" under US Customers**

#### International Customers
- You're outside the United States
- Will receive prepaid shipping label
- Shipping timeline: Within 10 days of receiving label

**Click "Select" under International Customers**

### Step 3: Review Guidelines

Read the return process carefully:

1. **45-Day Window**: Submit within 45 days of invoice
2. **2-Day Review**: We review within 2 business days
3. **Wait for Approval**: Don't ship until approved
4. **Pack Carefully**: Use proper packaging
5. **Credit Schedule**: Credits processed on Sundays

**Click "Proceed to RMA Form"** when ready

### Step 4: Fill Out the Form

Enter your information:

#### Company Name
```
Example: Tech Solutions Inc
```
- Full legal company name
- Must match invoice

#### Company Email
```
Example: returns@techsolutions.com
```
- Valid email address
- We'll send updates here
- Check spam folder for responses

#### Sales Order Number
```
Example: SO-2025-001
```
- From your original invoice
- Starts with "SO-"
- If you don't have it, contact us

#### Quantity to Return
```
Example: 50
```
- Number of devices in this RMA
- Must be between 1 and 10,000
- Should match your Excel file

### Step 5: Upload Files

**Required**: At least one file

#### Best Practice: Upload Excel File

**Why Excel**: We can automatically extract device details

**Format Your Excel File**:

| IMEI | Model | Storage | Grade | Issue | Issue Category | Repair/Return | Unit Price | Repair Cost |
|------|-------|---------|-------|-------|----------------|---------------|------------|-------------|
| 357069403525410 | iPhone 14 Pro | 256GB | B | Screen crack | Physical Damage | RETURN | 750 | |
| 357069403525411 | iPhone 14 | 128GB | A | Battery | Functional | REPAIR | 650 | 50 |

**Important**:
- IMEIs must be 15 digits
- Start with 35
- Use Text format in Excel (not General) to prevent scientific notation

#### Other Files You Can Upload

- **PDF**: Invoices, packing slips
- **Images**: Photos of devices (JPG, PNG)
- **Videos**: Videos showing issues (MP4, MOV)
- **CSV**: Alternative to Excel

#### How to Upload

**Method 1**: Drag and Drop
- Drag files from your computer
- Drop onto the upload area
- Files appear in list below

**Method 2**: Browse Button
- Click "Browse Files"
- Select files from your computer
- Click "Open"

**File Limits**:
- Maximum 10MB per file
- No limit on number of files
- All file types accepted

#### Removing Files

- Click **"Remove"** button next to any file
- File deleted from upload list
- Can re-add if needed

### Step 6: Submit

1. **Review your information**
   - Company name correct?
   - Email address correct?
   - Files uploaded?

2. **Click "Submit RMA Request"**
   - Button turns into loading spinner
   - Takes 5-30 seconds depending on file size
   - Don't close browser or refresh page

3. **Wait for confirmation**

- Use reference number in all communication

## Common Questions

### What file formats do you accept?

We accept all file types, but **Excel (.xlsx) or CSV** works best for device lists because we can automatically process them.

### Do my IMEIs need to be in a specific format?

Yes:
- Exactly 15 digits
- Start with 35
- Example: 357069403525410

**Excel Tip**: Format IMEI cells as "Text" not "General" to prevent Excel from converting to scientific notation.

### Can I submit multiple RMAs?

Yes! Submit separate RMAs for different orders or batches. Each gets its own reference number.

### How do I check my RMA status?

Currently: Email us at rma@scalmob.com with your reference number.

**Coming Soon**: Status tracking portal where you can check yourself.

### What if I made a mistake?

Email rma@scalmob.com immediately with:
- Your reference number
- What needs to be corrected
- Corrected information

### Can I cancel my RMA?

Yes, email rma@scalmob.com with your reference number before shipping devices.

### What if I'm outside the 45-day window?

Contact rma@scalmob.com to discuss. We may still be able to help depending on circumstances.

### What happens if I ship without approval?

**Don't do this!** Devices shipped without approved RMA may be:
- Rejected
- Returned to you at your expense
- Not eligible for credit

Always wait for approval email first.

## Troubleshooting

### Portal won't load

- Check your internet connection
- Try a different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari)
- Clear browser cache
- Contact rma@scalmob.com

### Files won't upload

- Check file size (max 10MB each)
- Try one file at a time
- Use different browser
- Contact rma@scalmob.com for alternative submission method

### Form won't submit

- Check all required fields are filled
- Verify email address format
- Make sure at least one file is uploaded
- Try refreshing page and re-entering

### Didn't receive reference number

- Check if page showed success message (might have missed it)
- Check spam/junk folder for confirmation email
- Contact rma@scalmob.com with approximate submission time

### Didn't receive approval email

Wait 2 full business days, then:
- Check spam/junk folder
- Check email address was correct
- Contact rma@scalmob.com with reference number
